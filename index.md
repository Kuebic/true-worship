## The pieces

- **[Worship, in One Line]({{ '/worship-crystal' | relative_url }})** — the definition and its whole spine on one page. Pin it up.
- **[How Worship Grew Up]({{ '/worship-for-newcomers' | relative_url }})** — for anyone: how worship matures from servant, to child, to true child, and why the last step is a coming of age rather than a break from your past.
- **[Worship for Music Directors]({{ '/worship-for-music-directors' | relative_url }})** — for those who lead: sorting the inherited definition of worship from the truth, so you know what you're doing on stage, why, and how.
